Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 11nd, 2017.

I do not place any advertisements or trackers on This Website.

You own the copyright to Comments, and You may request to remove Comments, at any time for any reason.

I will never disclose, share, or sell, any Comments, with any Third Party, for the purpose of conducting any Business Transaction.

I will not voluntarily share any Comments with Law Enforcement, unless I believe there is a Serious or Credible Threat of You causing any Bodily or Emotional Harm for any reason.

If there is a request to give Comments to Law Enforcement, I will delete Comments, without any advanced warning to You, so long as I face no Legal Penalties or Repercussions for deleting Comments.

I will comply with requests to give Comments to Law Enforcement if I face any Criminal Penalties, any Civil Penalties, or any Financial Penalties for refusing to cooperate with Law Enforcement, or if there is a legal court order by a judge compelling me to turn over Comments, or if there there is any other Legal Authority with a legal warrant that legally compels Me to give Comments to a Legal Authority.

I reserve the right to remove any Comments, for any reason and at any time, without any advanced warning to You of My intentions to do so.

I reserve the right to completely change or amend the Privacy Policy for This Website, for any reason and at any time, without any advanced warning to You of My intentions to do so.
